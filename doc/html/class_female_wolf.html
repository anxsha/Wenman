Detailed Description

A female wolf prioritizes making a move that allows it to eat a bunny. If not possible, chooses a random neighbouring square. When a male wolf moves to a square with a female one (and w/o a bunny), the female gets pregnant (unless already had been) for the next 2 turns. Then gives birth to a wolf of random sex, passing them its fat value.
